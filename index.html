Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this action-packed adventure game!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must defeat endless hordes of monsters. Choose your character, unlock powerful abilities, and see how long you can survive against increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your character attacks automatically. Collect experience by defeating enemies to level up and choose new abilities. Survive as long as possible and try to reach the highest level!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Unlock and play as different characters with unique abilities and playstyles.

Skill Tree

Level up and choose from a variety of skills to create your own build.

Enemy Variety

Face different types of monsters with varying attack patterns and abilities.

Power-ups

Collect items throughout the game to enhance your character's strength.

Tips for Success

Focus on movement and positioning to avoid getting surrounded by enemies. Prioritize abilities that complement each other for maximum effectiveness. Don't forget to collect health power-ups when your health is running low.
